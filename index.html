Genome Explorer® is a comprehensive cloud-based clinical genomics platform, which enables precision medicine through evidence-based genomic assessment of cancers and inherited disorders. It is an enterprise genomics software system to analyze and interpret sequence data from Illumina and Ion torrent sequencing platforms and supports major analysis pipelines including whole-genome sequencing, whole-exome sequencing, targeted gene panel analysis, pharmacogenomics, gene fusions, CNVs, SVs and TMB/MSI analysis. It is a comprehensive platform with automated workflows that provides innovative, cost-effective, and readily accessible Clinical Decision Support (CDS) simply from a web browser. Genome Explorer® incorporates the standard practice guidelines from AMP, ACMG, CAP, and NCCN for interpreting and reporting the sequence variants. The actionable clinical report includes drugs approved by FDA, EMA, HCSC and PMDA, clinical trials, drug dosage, side effects, and drug-metabolizing phenotypes as per CPIC guidelines.
